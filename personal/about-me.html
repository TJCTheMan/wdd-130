Call me TJ

My name is Tevin Coles, I am 23 years old and I am from Ririe, Idaho. I am a Linux Enthusiast, Content Creator, Gamer/Streamer, and I dabble in programming and Web development. I have been building PC's since I was in high school, I was administrating a rather large modded Minecraft server and accompanying Discord server at the time, and due to my limited amount of ram, was introduced to Ubuntu as a lighter-weight operating system that would allow me to run bigger modpacks better. This is how I got into Linux in the first place.

Around the time I graduated, I was called to serve a mission for the church in Dallas, Texas. I retured home in October 2019, and shortly thereafter, built the first iteration of the system I run today. That first "real gaming rig" was a ryzen 5 2600, 16GB of DDR4 (quickly upgraded to 32), and an RX580 8GB, all on a ASUS ROG STRIX B450-F Gaming Motherboard. This was also my introduction to Windows 10, as well as Solid-State Drives.

I ran this system for around a year, but as time went on, things got replaced and upgraded. I now have a much better PC that i am still upgrading and improving, and have been dualbooting Windows and Linux on for about 2 years now. I am now at BYU-I, taking classes for my Major, Computer Engineering. I still admin for a few minecraft servers, I am a content creator with the online handle of TJCTheMan, if you want to know more, there are links and things over at TJCTheMan.com. Thanks for taking some time to read this and I hope to get to know all of you better as time goes on.
